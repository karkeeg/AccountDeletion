Account Deletion Request

⚠️ Important Notice: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account profile and personal information
  • All rankings and tracking data
  • App preferences and settings
  • Login credentials and authentication data
  • Any associated files or documents
  • Usage analytics and activity logs

Data Retention Policy

After your deletion request is processed:

  • Most data is deleted immediately
  • Some backup data may remain for up to 30 days for system recovery purposes
  • Legal compliance data may be retained as required by law
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for service improvement

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your FirstRank account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Verify Your Identity: Send an email from the email address associated with your FirstRank account
  2. Email Subject: "Account Deletion Request - FirstRank Mobile"
  3. Include the Following Information:
    • Your full name (as registered in the app)
    • Email address associated with your account
    • Username or User ID (if known)
    • Reason for deletion (optional)
  4. Send Email To: aidotechindia@gmail.com

Processing Timeline

  • Confirmation: We will confirm receipt within 24 hours
  • Identity Verification: 1-2 business days
  • Data Deletion: Completed within 7 business days
  • Final Confirmation: You will receive confirmation when deletion is complete
